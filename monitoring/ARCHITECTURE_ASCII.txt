K3s (single-node)
└── monitoring (namespace)
    ├── Prometheus (kube-prometheus-stack)
    │   ├── prometheus
    │   ├── alertmanager
    │   └── node-exporter
    ├── Grafana
    └── Loki + Promtail
Logs ---> Promtail ---> Loki ---> Grafana
Metrics ---> kube-state-metrics / node-exporter ---> Prometheus ---> Grafana
Alerts ---> Prometheus Alertmanager ---> (Email/Slack) (optional)
